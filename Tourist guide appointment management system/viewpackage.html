MONTE RICHIE BEACH RESORT TOUR PACKAGE
Experience the tranquility of Monte Richie Beach Resort, the perfect seaside getaway where golden beaches meet blue waves. Exquisite lodging, fine eating, and exhilarating experiences are what await, guaranteeing a memorable vacation. Come relax and experience the beauty of being by the sea like never before.

Location:

Barangay San Antonio, Jasaan, Misamis Oriental (along the highway)

Tour Guide:

Palagtiw , Angeline(09559811263)

Duration:

30-40 minutes away from the city

Price:

PHP 75 for entrance and pool access. For room check-in accomodation and karaoke rental, you may ask the front desk staff.

SOPHE RED HOTEL & ONSHORE RESTAURANT TOUR PACKAGE
For those looking for luxury, adventure, and relaxation, the Sophie Red Hotel is the perfect weekend retreat. The hotel, which is tucked away along the lovely coastline, provides lovely views of the ocean, calm surroundings, and opulent lodging. The Sophie Red Hotel offers the ideal environment for a restorative getaway,enjoy fine dining at Onshore Restaurant, or simply relax by the pool. With impeccable service, world-class amenities, and a tranquil atmosphere, guests can truly disconnect from the hustle and bustle of everyday life and create lasting memories in a serene paradise..

Location:

Barangay Bobontugan , Jasaan, Misamis Oriental (along the highway)

Tour Guide:

Bitos, Ivy(09559811263)

Duration:

30-40 minutes away from the city

Price:

PHP 1,200 banana boat rental(good for 6 person) and PHP 100 (pool entrance). For room check-in accomodation you may ask the front desk receptionist.
